R(e)-thrift

R(e)-thrift is a place for people to buy & sell lightly used goods. We offer an easy way to browse, purchase, and sell items, with or without an account. If you have an account, your shopping cart can be seen on any of your devices.

Built with: PostgreSQL, Node.js, Express, React, Bootstrap CSS, Stripe API
